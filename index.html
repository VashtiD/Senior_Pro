Description:

Our team developed MindTune, an emotional wellness app designed to support mental health through daily mood tracking, self-care habits, and interactive features. The app helps users become more aware of their emotions, encourages healthy routines like journaling, hydration, and virtual plant care, and visualizes emotional patterns over time. We faced challenges in creating a responsive design across devices but overcame them by reorganizing layouts and ensuring smooth navigation. Using Figma, we planned and visualized each screen for a seamless user experience.
